About Us

Management

The school functions under the daily supervision, guidance and administration of the Correspondent Mr. Jayabarathan Chelliah who holds a Master’s degree in Educational Administration from Loma Linda University, California, U.S.A. He has more than 30 years of service as the head of reputed English medium schools. In recognition of his contribution to the cause of education the Government of Tamil Nadu has awarded him the Dr. Radhakrishnan Award. The Mount Zion Christian Educational Trust also has educationists whose decades of experience and expertise in the field of education will make learning a joy for your children. The school will be under the careful supervision and guidance of Mrs. Florence Jayabarathan who had more than 30 years of service will brighten the school atmosphere and encourage teachers and students alike to reach greater and greater heights. She has already contributed significantly to tremendous growth and development of Mount Zion Matriculation Higher Secondary school. Probably this is the only school in the state where all the managing team of the school are qualified and experienced teachers.

MR. JAYABARATHAN CHELLIAH, M.A., B.ED.,

CORRESPONDENT

Mr. Jayabarathan Chelliah, the founder and Correspondent of the school, is the Managing Trustee of the Mount Zion Christian Education Trust. He holds a bachelor’s degree in science from Alagappa University, Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu, a bachelor’s degree in education from Calicut University, Kerala. He graduated in June, 1978 with a master’s degree in Educational administration from the Loma Linda University, California, U.S.A. He is also the founder and Chairman of Mount Zion College of Engineering and Technology at Pudukkottai, Tamil Nadu.

MRS.FLORENCE JAYABARATHAN, M.A., B.ED.,

CO-FOUNDER

Trustee, Mrs. Florence Jayabarathan is the co-founder of the school along with her husband. She is the Headmistress of the school since 1988. Under her able leadership, the very first batch of +2, which sat for the public exam in 1994, bagged the I place in the District.

DR. JONATHAN JAYABARATHAN, M.A., B.ED., PH.D

TRUSTEE

Trustee, Dr. Jonathan Preeth Jayabarathan is the Senior Principal of the school. He holds a bachelor’s degree in science from American College, Madurai, a bachelor’s degree in education and a master’s degree in English from Bharathidasan University, Trichy and a Ph.D degree from Tamil University.
