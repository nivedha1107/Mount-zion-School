Salient Features Of Mount Zion School

Our vision is to help the young develop into upright, creative and loyal citizens, imbued with love for God and compassion and concern for others and nature, with spiritual sensitivity and a sense of personal freedom and to equip them to earn a living and contribute to the economic and social progress of the nation and integrity of creation.

Attractive surrounding that facilitate interpersonal interaction.

A modern Educational approach to the individual

Enhanced smart board facility.

Individual attention from fully qualified professional teachers.

NIE (Newspaper in Education) from class V.

Field trips as an extension of classroom learning.

Clean, safe and separate toilets for girls and boys to ensure clean sanitation facilities.

Faculty members are always available to provide information and guidance to the students about higher education and careers.

Experiential learning in well-equipped laboratories.

Eminent guest speakers from various parts of the world with varied expertise

Extensive library resources.

A balanced Co-curricular activity.

Excellent transport facility.

Regular availability of purified drinking water.
